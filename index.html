About Mithilakirti Naturals

Mithilakirti Naturals Private Limited is a premium supplier of high-quality Makhana (Fox Nuts) sourced directly from the fertile Mithila region of Bihar, India — the global heartland of authentic makhana cultivation.

We work closely with local farmers to ensure that every batch of makhana is carefully harvested, naturally dried, and processed while maintaining its purity, crunch, and nutritional value.

Our mission is to bring the authentic taste and health benefits of Mithila makhana to customers across India and international markets while supporting sustainable agriculture and local farming communities.

At Mithilakirti Naturals, we believe in purity, quality, and trust — delivering nature’s finest superfood directly from farm to table.
